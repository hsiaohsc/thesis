向量圖形非寫實電腦計算繪製

  • 進入系統

摘要



圖形的非寫實計算繪製是電腦圖學領域中相當成熟的技術，有許多將現實虛擬化的種種應用。伴隨著可微分光柵化器的技術出現，使得不能進行卷積的向量結構也能夠利用梯度下降的方式進行機器學習。


Non-photorealistic rendering of graphics is a well-established technique in the field of computer graphics, with various applications that virtualize reality. The advent of differentiable rasterizer has enabled machine learning with gradient descent for vector structures that were previously unable to undergo convolution.


本研究針對浮世繪代表的《冨嶽三十六景》，探索其進行向量非寫實電腦計算繪製的可能性。透過可微分光柵化器，遍歷關於構成向量圖的形狀選擇、形狀的數量以及迭代的次數所生成的向量非寫實電腦計算繪製結果向量圖，並進一步產生其變化的動畫可縮放向量圖。


This study explores the possibility of applying vector non-photorealistic computer rendering to the Japanese art series "Thirty-Six Views of Mount Fuji". Through the differentiable rasterizer, we explore the impact of shape selection, the number of shapes composing the vector image, and the iteration count on the generated results of non-photorealistic rendered vector graphics. Furthermore, we produce animated scalable vector images to show the whole generating process.


實驗結果證實，使用可微分光柵化器進行向量非寫實電腦計算繪製的結果，在色彩表現上遠勝於傳統向量化方法。除此之外，實驗結果也表現出向量非寫實電腦計算繪製在生成任務上無論是不同圖片大小、色彩與圖片結構的泛用性。


Experimental results confirm that using the differentiable rasterizer for non-photorealistic rendered vector graphics significantly outperforms traditional algorithm based vectorization methods in terms of color representation. Moreover, the experimental results also demonstrate the broad applicability of non-photorealistic rendered vector graphics in the generation task, regardless of different image sizes, colors, and image structures.
